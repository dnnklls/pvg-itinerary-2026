Before you land

Logistics.

Phone and apps

Google, WhatsApp, and Instagram are blocked. Turn on roaming or an eSIM — roaming data bypasses the firewall — or have a VPN ready before you land. Use Apple Maps or Amap, and Didi for rides.

Paying for things

Near-cashless city. Link a card to Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival — most stalls and taxis won't take foreign cards. Keep a little cash as backup.

Late-June weather

Plum-rain season: hot, sticky, 28–32°C with sudden downpours. Pack a compact umbrella and light, breathable clothes. The skyline looks just as good after rain.

Getting around

Metro Line 7 is at the door — add the metro QR in Alipay. Didi is cheap and works in English via the Alipay mini-program. Station rule: metered taxis only, and don't drink the tap water.

Crew perks

Happy hour 1–6pm, 25% off food and drink with crew ID, free shuttle to Sanchenli Mall at 11:00 and 18:20, and the Lobby Bar crew lounge. The front desk handles your wake-up call.
