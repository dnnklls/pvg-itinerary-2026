Didi or Metro to Lujiazui (~20–25 min). This is the cluster of giants: Shanghai Tower, the SWFC "bottle-opener", Jin Mao and the Oriental Pearl all in a few blocks. Wander the elevated walkway ring to feel the scale before going up.

🌉 Riverside walk + the Bund glow

Down at street level, stroll the Binjiang Avenue promenade along the water. From the Pudong bank you get the postcard: the lit-up colonial Bund facing you, Oriental Pearl blazing behind. If you've energy, hop Line 2 one stop under the river to East Nanjing Rd and walk onto the Bund itself.

FreeBest after 7:30pm, lights full on

Before you go

Know before you land

📱 Phone & apps

Google, WhatsApp, Instagram are blocked in China. Turn on roaming or an eSIM (roaming data bypasses the firewall), or have a VPN ready before you land. For maps use Apple Maps or Amap; for rides, Didi.

💳 Paying for things

It's a near-cashless city. Link a card to Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival — most stalls and taxis won't take foreign cards. Keep a little cash as backup.

🌧️ Late-June weather

This is plum-rain season (梅雨): hot, sticky, 28–32°C with sudden downpours. Pack a compact umbrella and light, breathable clothes. The skyline is just as good after rain.

🚇 Getting around

Metro Line 7 is at your doorstep; add the metro QR in Alipay. Didi is cheap and English-friendly via the Alipay mini-program. Station rule: metered taxis only, and don't drink the tap water.

🏨 Crew perks

Happy hour 1–6pm, 25% off F&B with crew ID, free shuttle to Sanchenli Mall (11:00 & 18:20), and the Lobby Bar crew lounge. Front desk handles your wake-up call.
